We're building pressureNET

We want a true, accurate weather forecast. Big meteorology companies are satisfied with 80% accuracy.

Through pressureNET we're collecting air pressure readings from phones all over the world. We're collaborating with researchers to develop weather models that user our readings to create forecasts.

If you have a barometer in your Android device, help science out and get the app! If you're a developer or researcher, check out the tools we have for you here.
